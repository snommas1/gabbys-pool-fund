🌟 Gabby's Story

Hey everyone! 👋

You all know Gabby — the little girl who turns 7 this summer and has exactly one birthday wish. Not toys. Not a party at a bounce place. Not even a cake shaped like a unicorn (though she's open to that as a bonus).

Gabby wants a pool.

She sat us all down and said — completely seriously — "I don't want a bunch of stuff. I want a pool so I can be a mermaid every single day this summer."

And honestly? We couldn't argue with that. ☀️ You've all been to Tucson in June. You know what it's like. This kid deserves to splash.

The big idea is simple — one gift, one pool, one very happy kid. 🏊‍♀️

And yes — there are rewards. Gabby personally designed the thank-you tiers. She watched one episode of Shark Tank and has been calling everyone her "investors" ever since. We may have created a monster. 🦈

Every dollar — big or small — brings us closer to cannonball season. The goal is to have the pool delivered and ready by the first week of June, so we need to move fast!

Let's make this the summer Gabby never forgets. 💖

With love,
— The Whole Family 🫶
